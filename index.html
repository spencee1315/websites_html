What is HTML & What is HTML5?

HTML stands for hypertext markup language and
it is the foundational piece to your website.
It is the actual content you want on your website.

HTML5 was released in 2014 and is the fifth version of HTML.
It was released as a W3C recommendation, bringing the
specification process to complete.

Just a few of the items that can be included in your website using HTML:

  • Links
  • Pictures
  • Blog Articles
  • Navigation
